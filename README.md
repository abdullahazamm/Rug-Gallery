# Rug Gallery

**Project Link**: [https://ruggallery.net/](https://ruggallery.net/)

## Description
For this project, I developed a custom **Shopify e-commerce website** for **Rug Gallery**, a rug brand based in the United States. The website was built from scratch, focusing on a seamless user experience and flexible product options.

Key tasks included:
- **Shopify Theme Design**: Created a custom Shopify theme from scratch and customized the **Liquid code** to meet the clientâ€™s requirements.
- **Custom Product Pages**: Designed and implemented flexible product options to allow customers to customize their rug selections based on size, material, color, and other attributes.
- **Bulk Product Upload**: Handled the bulk upload of product listings to streamline the product population process.
- **Payment Gateway Integration**: Integrated **Shopify Payments** and **PayPal** to provide secure payment options for customers.
- **Behavioral Analysis**: Integrated **Microsoft Clarity**, **Google Analytics**, and **Google Tag Manager** for customer behavior analysis, enabling the business to track user interactions, identify pain points, and optimize the sales funnel.
- **Documentation**: Worked with the business development team to document the website proposal and successfully secure the project.

### Technologies Used:
- **Shopify**
- **Liquid**
- **PayPal** for payment gateway
- **Microsoft Clarity** for customer behavioral analysis
- **Google Analytics**
- **Google Tag Manager**
